# Skill: Memory Stacking

**Source:** "5 Claude Skills That Make You Unstoppable At Work"
**Use when:** You have any recurring workflow with Claude — weekly reports, client communications, strategy documents, internal memos — where you find yourself re-explaining the same context every time.

## The Problem

Without Projects or custom instructions, every conversation with Claude starts from zero. Every time you ask for something, you have to re-explain your role, your company, your industry, your writing preferences, and the context of what you're working on. That setup takes 5–10 minutes per conversation. Over a week of daily use, that's over an hour spent just re-explaining who you are.

## The Instruction

Set up a Claude Project and load it with your persistent context, including:
- Your role and responsibilities
- Your company's products and market position
- Your preferred writing tone and format
- Any recurring templates or frameworks you use
- The names and roles of key stakeholders you reference often
- Your company's specific terminology or acronyms

## Why It Works

Once that context is loaded, every conversation inside the project starts with Claude already knowing who you are, what you do, and how you like things done. It's the difference between briefing a new contractor every single time and handing a task to someone who's been on your team for six months.

A weekly reporting workflow that used to take 20 minutes of context-setting and back-and-forth can shrink to a single message — paste in the raw metrics, type "weekly report," and get a draft back in your format and tone using the metrics you always track.

Use separate projects for separate contexts — for example, client communications, strategy documents, and internal memos — so each one carries its own context and the output matches the audience without you specifying it every time.

## Why This Compounds the Other Four Skills

Memory stacking makes every other skill faster and sharper:
- **The Interrogation Method** asks better questions because Claude already knows your business.
- **The Perspective Shift** reviews from more accurate angles because it knows your stakeholders by name.
- **Chain of Documents** produces more relevant research because it knows your industry and competitive landscape.
- **The Breakdown Method** identifies more accurate dependencies because it knows your team structure and approval processes.

Used together, these five skills compound — your process gets faster every time you use Claude.
